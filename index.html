COVID-19 Wealth vs. Health

Due to COVID-19 having been shown to spread more in communities rife with poverty according to certain studies, I decided to compare how these factors played out in New York City (NYC) using data from the 2017 ACS for household income, as well as data provided by NYC for the COVID-19 death rates through their github.
